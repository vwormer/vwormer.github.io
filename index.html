Before, I worked as an Online Editor at the German local newspaper Heilbronner Stimme, where we realized several data driven and interactive stories (e.g. this, this and that).

I also was Rudolf Augstein data fellow at the German investigative office CORRECT!V in Berlin, where I've learned a lot about investigative reporting and the German "Informationsfreiheitsgesetz" (Freedom of Information). I still contribute to a data based research regarding elderly homes.

In my free time I join meetings of the local open data community Code for Germany. Further, I write about data journalism and other media stuff in my blog #DIGILAB and speak about these topics at conferences.
